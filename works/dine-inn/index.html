Dine Inn

Dine Inn is a home-chef community that aims to connect makers of food to lovers of food.

Since their inception in 2016, the platform has only undergone minor changes to iron out the teething troubles.

Fast-forward to 2021, with a growing number of users, it was time for a design overhaul to boost conversion rates and to add new features.

Role

UI / UX Designer

Deliverables

Desktop and Mobile Prototypes

The Problem

To meet the needs of their evergrowing community, Dine Inn has constantly added new features to their platform.

As many of these new features were not considered in the initial design, it was difficult to implement the new features well. As a result, the user experience of the platform worsened.

The visual design was also starting to feel out of date relative to its competitors.
